My Product · Founder

I'm the founder of BikeCare —
the AI-powered bike maintenance tracker.

Client work is further down. First, what I built as a solo founder: a native iOS and Android app with PWA offline mode, GDPR-compliant cloud backup, and an AI that knows when your chain is done.

„Your bike won't tell you when the chain is worn. We will.“

BikeCare auto-detects your bike with AI, forecasts wear parts in kilometers instead of months, and walks you through every repair step by step — from chain to derailleur.

  • AI auto-setup (Claude + Perplexity)
  • 20+ wear parts monitored
  • km-based maintenance forecasts
  • Strava sync + multi-bike garage
  • Step-by-step repair tutorials
  • PWA offline & GDPR cloud backup
Product, strategy and taste are mine. The code ships with Claude Code — the 2026 solo-founder playbook.
